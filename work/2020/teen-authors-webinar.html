Teen Authors Webinar

Saturday, October 17, 2020 | 4:00 - 5:00pm EDT (1:00 - 2:00 pm PDT)

Presented by Triangle Writers Studio and Young Modern Minds Inc.

Join us for an hour long webinar featuring two published teen authors, Sarah Frank and Ariana Tosado!
In this info-packed, hour long webinar, they will discuss:

  • The process of becoming a teen author
  • Struggles they faced along the way
  • What their books are about
  • How they overcome writer's block
  • Advice for young writers and aspiring authors
